About Me

Ever since I was a child, I have always wanted to develop videogames of my own, and since I got my first computer I have always found myself intrigued by how they work. With the support of my computer science teacher, I found my love of programming and since then, I've not only wanted to make my own videogames, but I've also wanted to develop my own useful software. Now that I'm in university, I want to continue my love of computer science and allow myself to program videogames and software to the best of my ability. However, I also have ONE major wish for myself which is to never make weapons or allow my programs to cause harm.


Beyond my love of computer science, I also have a great fondness for the following:

-Photography: I have a camera with me most of the time so that I can take pictures of the landscapes that me and my family go to. While I do edit images, I prefer to keep them as close to their original versions as possible.



-Video Editing: I love making videos of various things (usually video games or my programs) and editing them for people to watch. Most of my videos would be quite boring for most people to watch, but I edit them because I love video editing.



-Writing: I love writing various stories, especially short ones. One day, I would love to be able to publish my own book, but I can never find my stories good enough and usually stop writing them and start new ones.


And yes, I do love the colour orange. Blue is good too I guess. Oh yeah, and I'm not too great at CSS so I hope you enjoy the look of this site! It's probably the best I can do! -_-
